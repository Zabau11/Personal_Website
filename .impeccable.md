## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are potential clients, collaborators, and hiring stakeholders evaluating David's work through a fast personal website scan. They need quick confidence in capability, clarity, and execution quality.

### Brand Personality
Calm, precise, trustworthy. The interface should feel intentional, focused, and technically reliable without visual noise.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark monochrome-first presentation with mostly black and white tones, subtle neutral contrast, and restrained accents only where needed for hierarchy or interaction clarity.

### Design Principles
- Prioritize legibility and clear hierarchy over decorative color.
- Use a disciplined black/white-neutral palette with minimal accent usage.
- Keep interactive states obvious through contrast, not saturation.
- Preserve a clean editorial rhythm with consistent surface and border treatment.
- Maintain accessibility-first contrast for text, controls, and focus states.
