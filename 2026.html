2026 So Far

April 19, 2026

2026 has hit the ground running with incredible intensity. The year began with my first major concept, Clearize, which I built using a mix of logic and "vibe coding." Exploring this new workflow has been a revelation, and the project is now officially entering the home stretch. With the launch scheduled for the next 10-15 days, the nerves are there, but as the saying goes, if you don't try, you'll never know. This journey has solidified my passion for building accessible AI solutions for people who aren't yet familiar with the technology.

On the personal side, I recently crossed a major milestone by completing my first official race: a 10k in Cluj-Napoca. It was an unforgettable experience. The entire training block taught me the discipline of setting a high-stakes goal and the grit required to see it through to the finish line.

Now, I am channeling that momentum into a new project: a platform designed to revolutionize how high school students in Romania learn Computer Science. While that might sound like a bold claim, the mission is clear, and I am aiming to launch the MVP within the next two months.

It's been an action-packed year so far, and I'm ready for whatever comes next.
